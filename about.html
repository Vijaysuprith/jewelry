Our Story

Crafting memories, one diamond at a time.

The Lumière Philosophy

Founded in 2026, Lumière Jewelry was born from a passion for timeless elegance and modern craftsmanship. We believe that jewelry is more than just an accessory; it is a personal statement, a cherished memory, and a legacy to be passed down.

Our artisans combine traditional techniques with contemporary design to create pieces that are both unique and enduring. We source only the finest materials, ensuring that every gemstone and every gram of gold meets our rigorous standards of quality.

Ethical Sourcing

We are committed to conflict-free sourcing. Our diamonds and gemstones are traced from the mine to the market, ensuring fair labor practices and environmental responsibility.

Master Craftsmanship

Each piece is finished by hand in our New York atelier. Our master jewelers dedicate hours to perfecting every setting, ensuring your jewelry lasts a lifetime.
